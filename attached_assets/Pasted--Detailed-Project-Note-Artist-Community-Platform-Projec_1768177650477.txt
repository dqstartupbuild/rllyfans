# Detailed Project Note: Artist Community Platform

## Project Overview

A Next.js application that enables music artists to build and monetize exclusive communities with their biggest fans through subscription-based access.

## Homepage & Discovery

The domain homepage functions as a community browser where users can:

- Browse and scroll through a list of available artist communities
- View basic information about each community (title, description, and essential details)
- **Note:** Non-members cannot see actual community content, posts, or interactions until they subscribe

## User Access & Monetization

### Artist Accounts

- **Free sign-up** for all artists
- Artists receive subscription proceeds minus a small platform service fee
- Full control over their community content and engagement

### Fan Accounts

- **Free sign-up** for all fans
- Access to communities requires a **paid subscription** (monthly or yearly options)
- Subscriptions are community-specific (per artist)

## Artist Features & Content Sharing

Artists can engage their community through multiple content types:

- **Live streaming** capabilities
- **Video and image sharing**
- **Text posts** for updates and announcements
- **Music sharing** with streaming and download options for members
- **Audio spaces** (similar to Twitter Spaces) for live audio conversations

## Fan/User Features

Community members can:

- Post to the community timeline
- Comment on posts from artists and other members
- Like and interact with content
- Participate in discussions and community conversations
- Join artist-hosted audio spaces

## Profile Customization

### Artist Profiles

- Standard music artist profile layout
- Professional presentation of artist information

### User Profiles

Users can customize their profiles with:

- Profile picture uploads
- List of favorite artists
- Personal hobbies and interests
- Other biographical information

## Technical Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js

-----

*This platform creates a direct artist-to-fan relationship through exclusive, subscription-based communities with comprehensive content sharing and social interaction features.*
